About Us

Toter is a trash can and dumpster manufacturer that offers various products for homeowners, municipalities, and waste haulers. The company prides itself on the durability and convenience of its products. They also offer services such as cart assembly, delivery, and maintenance.

Read More

Established over 50 years ago, Toter has been at the forefront of innovation in waste management, known for its industry-leading design and heavy-duty materials. Our products are eco-friendly and designed to last longer than typical carts, reducing overall environmental impact.

We cater to various sectors, including residential, municipal, and industrial clients, ensuring that every need is met with quality and professionalism. Our signature products, such as two-wheel and caster carts, are engineered for strength and impact resistance.

Toter is committed to sustainability, utilizing recycled materials wherever possible in the production of our waste carts. Our innovative solutions help improve waste management systems globally.
